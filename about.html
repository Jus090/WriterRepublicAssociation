About Us

Welcome to Writer Republic Association

The Writers Republic Association (WRA) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) established in 2020 by Joshua Nnadimma Ayozie, a writer, poet, and philosopher. Recognizing a gap in the area of promoting reading and writing as fundamental skills, WRA aims to encourage and improve literacy, particularly in its local community. Initially a social media group, WRA transitioned into a formal organization in 2023. Through bi-monthly meetings and reading sessions, WRA identified dedicated members who now form the Board of Trustees. The association is officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria as an incorporated trustee NGO.
